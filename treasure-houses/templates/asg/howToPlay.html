Pick a Treasure House first to discover the treasure chests inside!


There are 10 chests in a room of Treasure House.


Now you can take actions: Open a chest or move to next room?

Open a chest will cost 1 key and Go to next room will cost 2 keys.


Chests only can be opnened from left to the right and one by one.


The game stats bar will display the useful information to you.


Once you used up all the keys, a result will pop over.


Get a 3 star will unlock next Treasure House!


Now, trying to find gems as many as you can!
