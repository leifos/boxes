Instruction



  • There are many rooms inside a treasure house, each room contains 10 treasure chests.


  • Your goal is to find as many gems inside treasure chests as you can.


  • Going to next room will bring back new treasure chests.


  • Opening chest will bring you gems.


  • Both of above actions will cost keys.


  • However, you only have a fixed number of keys in each game.
